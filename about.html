WHO ARE WE

Solid waste management is the process of collecting, transporting, recovering, processing, and disposing of waste materials in an efficient and eco-friendly manner. It involves multiple stages, including waste collection, transportation to processing facilities, recovery of recyclable materials, and disposal through methods like composting, anaerobic digestion, incineration, or landfill. Effective waste management helps reduce environmental pollution, conserves natural resources, and promotes sustainable living.
